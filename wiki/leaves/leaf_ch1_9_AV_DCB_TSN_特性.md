<h level="2">AV、DCB、TSN 特性</h>

<info>
  <p>Chapter: [[产品概述]]</p>
  <p>Source: <code>QA enrichment</code></p>
  <p>Confidence: <code>high</code></p>
</info>

<p>AV (Audio Video): 100/1000 Mbps 下独立队列，最多8个 Rx queue / 7个 Tx queue，支持 IEEE 802.1-Qav credit-based shaper (CBS)，可编程 Slot Interval (1-4096 us)。DCB (Data Center Bridging): 100/1000 Mbps 下最多8个 Tx/Rx queue，支持 WRR/DWRR/WFQ/SP 调度，PFC (Priority-based Flow Control)。TSN (Time Sensitive Networking): IEEE 802.1Qbv (EST), IEEE 802.1Qbu/802.3br (Frame Preemption)。</p>
