# DWC_ether_qos 标准合规性

> Chapter: [[产品概述]]  
> Source: `QA enrichment`  
> Confidence: `high`

DWC_ether_qos 支持以下标准：IEEE 802.3-2015 (Ethernet MAC, GMII, MII, TBI); IEEE 1588-2008 (precision networked clock synchronization); IEEE 802.1AS-2011 / 802.1-Qav-2009 (AV traffic); IEEE 802.3az-2010 (Energy Efficient Ethernet); IEEE 802.1Qbv-2015 / 802.1Qbu-2016 (TSN); IEEE 802.1Qaz-2011 / 802.1Qbb-2011 (Data Center Bridging); RGMII v2.6, RMII v1.2, SGMII v1.8, SMII v2.1, RevMII。
